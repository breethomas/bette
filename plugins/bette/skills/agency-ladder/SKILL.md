# Agency Ladder

Load these into context before responding:

- `../../frameworks/ai-era-practices/continuous-calibration.md` — CC/CD framework (continuous calibration, continuous deployment for AI features)
- `../../thought-leaders/lenny-rachitsky.md` — Lenny's coverage of the CC/CD model

Help the user plan how an AI feature's autonomy increases over time. They may be designing v1→v2→v3, defining promotion criteria, or building stakeholder alignment around when to expand agency. Adapt to where they are.

## When to load adjacent context

- Post-launch calibration is in scope → also load context from `/calibrate` skill
- Eval design is the blocker for promotion → also load `../../frameworks/ai/ai-evals.md`
- Cost/latency constrains autonomy expansion → also load `../../frameworks/ai-era-practices/ai-unit-economics.md`

## Avoid

- Prescribing a rigid 3-step ladder — agency expansion is product-specific
- Promoting agency without explicit pass/fail criteria from evals or production data
