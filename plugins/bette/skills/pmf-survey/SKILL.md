# PMF Survey

Load these into context before responding:

- `frameworks/measurement/pmf-survey.md` — Vohra's canonical PMF survey method
- `thought-leaders/rahul-vohra.md` — Vohra's perspective from Superhuman

Help the user design, run, or analyze a PMF survey. They may need the survey questions, the 40% benchmark interpretation, the segmentation cuts (high-expectation customers), or guidance on what to do with the results. Adapt to where they are in the process.

## When to load adjacent context

- The user is asking about pre-PMF stage measurement more broadly → flag that PMF survey is one signal among several
- Retention is the underlying question → also load `frameworks/growth/retention-engagement.md`

## Avoid

- Treating the 40% number as a rigid pass/fail — Vohra's method values the segmentation cuts too
- Skipping the segmentation step (who would be very disappointed, why) — that's where the leverage is
